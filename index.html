What we do

Frazzled is a community based on the power of emotional openness. Founded by Ruby Wax in 2017, it's a space where anyone can come and be listened to, or simply listen, without judgment or solutions.

The simple act of opening up has the power to help not just the person sharing, but those we share with. This is a place where you can simply exhale and feel less alone in your feelings.

“I find it remarkable that this support is on offer and for free?! It's a unique, friendly, safe place, allowing me to feel less alone with my anxieties”

“Thanks for all you are doing - it’s making me happy and calming the chaos in my mind. Frazzled is a safe place filled with open ears, compassion, and healing.”

“In Frazzled sessions you get that instant raw human connection that brings out the true essence of what it means to be human.”
