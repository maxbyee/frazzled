Partner with us
Frazzled has partnerships with a range of remarkable organisations, and with each partnership, we build a bespoke model that is tailored to the organisation and their employees. Frazzled partnership packages are a wonderful way to increase your brand awareness and link your brand with a great initiative to support the mental health and wellbeing of your workforce.

In workplaces, the act of being vulnerable and sharing personal stories creates deep human connections and serves as a miraculous leveller quite unlike any other. When someone shares how they’re feeling, it resonates with others, and creates a chain reaction of honesty. Once people see each other’s vulnerability, a stronger working relationship and bond starts to flourish. As Ruby says, ‘it’s like the pressure leaves the pressure cooker’.

Frazzled is grateful to our partners and donors for their ongoing support in helping us provide a safe space for people to come and talk.
